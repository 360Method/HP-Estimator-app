### Tile Sizes
- 3x6 (subway), 4x4, 4x8, 6x6, 6x12
- 12x12, 12x24, 18x18, 24x24
- 24x48, 36x36 (large format)
- Mosaic: 1x1, 2x2 (on 12x12 sheet)

### Flooring
- Hardwood: 2.25", 3.25", 5", 7" wide planks
- LVP: 4", 5", 6", 7", 9" wide planks
- Carpet: sold per SF or SY (1 SY = 9 SF)

### Drywall Sheets
- 4x8, 4x10, 4x12 sheets (1/2", 5/8")

### Countertops
- Measured per SF (custom cut)
- Standard depth: 25.5" (bath), 25" (kitchen)

## Notes for HP Estimating
- Labor rates above are **fully burdened subcontractor billing rates** (not employee wages)
- HP markup on top of these rates per the hp-estimate-builder-v1 skill
- "Good" = builder-grade / functional quality
- "Better" = mid-range / most popular choice
- "Best" = premium / designer / high-end
- Portland metro labor is 15-25% above national average
- Licensed trades (plumbing, electrical) require permits in Portland
