# Roadmap follow-up cadence

This SOP is a descriptor: the work is done by the Lead Nurturer worker, not
the dispatcher. It is listed here so the SOP library shows the complete
picture of what the AI does on autopilot.

## What it does

When a homeowner submits the roadmap generator on handypioneers.com, the
Lead Nurturer schedules a follow-up drip (email/SMS drafts) according to the
`roadmap_followup` playbook stored in the `nurturer_playbooks` table. Drafts
are generated by `server/lib/leadNurturer/draftGenerator.ts`, surfaced in the
approvals inbox, and sent on approval (pre-approved copy may auto-send per
the Communication Engine rules).

## Where to change it

- Cadence steps, timing, and voice rules: the playbook editor in the admin
  UI (DB-backed, applies on next draft generation — no redeploy).
- Generation logic: `server/lib/leadNurturer/`.
- The 5-minute worker gate: `NURTURER_ENABLED` (defaults on).
